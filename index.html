Our Story

Rooted in Neapolitan Tradition

"Passione per la pizza"

Antico was born from a single conviction: that the world's greatest pizza deserves the world's finest ingredients, made with patience and respect for tradition. Chef Gioti built direct relationships with family-run producers across Italy — from the volcanic plains of Campania to the olive groves of Calabria.

Our San Marzano tomatoes are grown in the shadow of Mount Vesuvius. Our fior di latte comes from Agerola. Our olive oil is cold-pressed Kalamata. Nothing is compromised — ever.

Located just 120 metres from Leuven's Grote Markt, steps from Saint Peter's Church — Antico brings Napoli to Belgium's most vibrant university city.
